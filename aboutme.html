Education

Recent Graduate from the University of St Andrews

I graduated with a Master's in Mathematics from the University of St Andrews in 2024, having focused on pure mathematics and the applications of computer programming to mathematical problems. My dissertation was entitled 'A Walk along a Space-Filling Curve: Generating, Analysing, and Modeling Space-Filling Curves.' I analyzed space-filling curves by encoding them using Python and generating graphical representations of approximations to the curves, alongside more traditional mathematical analysis.

Coding

Experienced Professional and Passionate Hobbyist

I have a wide variety of professional programming experience. Specifically, I have worked with Skyscraper Foundry for the last few years, analyzing New York City's vast zoning data and creating an AI-assisted predictive planning tool. I have also worked with M. Arch Architects on their zoning app, Zoner, using Ruby on Rails to create the structure of the application. I also spent the last summer working with a team from the University of St Andrews to create an internal sustainability web application. In these projects, I have used everything from Python to Javascript to SQL.

I also enjoy working on personal programming projects. My game development work is explored further below, but on my own I do the Advent of Code, LeetCode, and spend time developing my personal Linux computer.

Video Game Development

Full Stack Game Development Team

I spend much of my free time working on video game development with SPRODSOFT, a team of three friends not counting myself. We have produced four games in the last three years: Box of Squingus (very well received!), Fishyphus of the Deep: Legend of the Eight Gems, Sprodkart, and Project Margrave.

Each of these games involved full stack development and many opportunities to learn and apply new skills, languages, and engines. Through this work I have improved my proficiency with C#, Godot, and Unity and learned about all different aspects of development, from back end to interface to 3D modeling.

Rowing

Trained Coxswain and Crew Member with Competition Experience

I am an experienced coxswain and rower and love working outdoors! I've rowed with the Village Community Boathouse for many years, rowing in their Sunday open sessions. I competed in high school with their youth crew, winning the 2019 Snow Row, the 2018 Icebreaker, and the 2018 Head of the Weir Race. I also occasionally join their annual Row Around Manhattan.

I also often participate in their boatbuilding sessions, helping to craft the historic Whitehall gigs and other boats used by the VCB, specifically the two boats Zoë and Integrity.

Box of Squingus

An innovative platformer spawned from Ludum Dare Game Jam #54. This game sees the player as Squingus, a charming but troubled cat, navigate the world from inside of its box, the titular Box of Squingus in this challenging and creative platforming challenge. Can the player transform Squingus' curse into a valuable asset?

Fishyphus of The Deep

A collectathon-style game spawned from Ludum Dare Game Jam #52. This game sees the player, a sockeye salmon, venture out into the dangerous ocean to bring back the legendary eight gems. With the power of the gems, the player has the chance to "turn the tide" as it were on the trawling nets that dangerously dredge The Deep.

SPRODKART

An innovative racing game, heavily inspired by Kirby Air Ride's City Trial mode. This game sees players competing in a variety of racing challenges, from drag races to agility courses to long distance jumps. Players ride in randomly spawned vehicles and collect randomly spawning power-ups to better their chances as the race goes on, and to prepare for the final race to determine a winner.

Using carbon nanospheres to reduce decoherence in quantum computing systems

I won second place nationally in the Exploravision science research paper competition for Grades 10-12, for my work on the above titled paper. The paper reviews existing research and investigates the properties of the carbon nanosphere, a particular formation of carbon, with exceptional electromagnetic stability, a feature that is essential to achievable quantum computing.
